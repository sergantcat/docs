## Project Highlights

These are some of the standout projects I'm most proud of across all categories. Each one pushed my skills in a new direction and taught me something valuable about game development and design.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="🏰 Obby Kingdom" icon="flag">
    A massive obstacle course game featuring over 200 unique stages spread across eight themed worlds — from lava caverns to sky castles. Built entirely in Roblox Studio with custom Blender props for each zone, Obby Kingdom became one of my most ambitious level design projects to date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🗺️ Fantasy RPG Map" icon="map">
    An expansive open-world environment set in a high-fantasy realm complete with forests, dungeons, and a central castle town. Every building and environmental asset was hand-modeled in Blender, then assembled and lit inside Roblox Studio to create an immersive atmosphere for RPG-style gameplay.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🛒 NexusHub Shop UI" icon="store">
    A fully animated in-game shop interface designed for a sci-fi themed experience. Features smooth tween animations, a tabbed category system, item preview panels, and a responsive layout that adapts cleanly for both PC and mobile players.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🌆 Sunrise City Roleplay" icon="city">
    A detailed town-simulation roleplay game featuring a working economy, job system, and dozens of unique interiors. From coffee shops to fire stations, every building was custom-built and furnished, creating a living, breathing city for players to explore and inhabit.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
